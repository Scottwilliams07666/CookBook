Chicken Noodle Soup

Source:Smitten Kitchen

"1 tablespoon vegetable oil", "1 large onion, chopped", "3-pound chicken, in parts or 3 pounds chicken pieces of your choice", "8 cups water", "1 bay leaf", "2 teaspoons table salt", "Freshly ground black pepper", "1 large carrot, diced (1/3-inch)", "1 medium parsnip, diced (1/3-inch) (optional)", "1 large celery stalk, diced (1/3-inch)", "3 ounces dried egg noodles, I prefer wide ones", "1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill or flat-leaf parsley"
